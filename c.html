CLINOMANIA

Meaning: An excessive desire to remain in bed; morbid sleepiness.

Usage:

  • Appetite and clinomania had turned him from a thin, wan, winsome youth into a bulging, flabby academic by the time he was thirty.
  • The word dysania means extreme difficulty in waking up and getting out of bed and clinomania is an obsessive desire to lie down.
  • No matter how much I sleep, it is as if my Clinomania is getting converted into insomnia.

SYNONYMNS ANTONYMNS
  • Dysania
  • Morning grogginess
  • Morning laziness
  • Morning sleepiness
  • Clinophobia
